# Prescreen — IT leadership roles

> **Status:** Skeleton. Replace with Peter's real client prescreens for CIO, Director IT, VP IT.

## When to use this

Distinct from tech-leadership (which is engineering/product). IT leadership = internal IT, corporate systems, security/compliance, infrastructure-for-the-business.

CIO, VP IT, Director of IT, Director of Information Security, Head of IT Operations, IT Manager (for mid-market roles).

## Standard questions

1. Walk me through your current IT org structure.
2. What's the budget you manage? Allocation across infra, software, services, headcount?
3. Major system migrations you've owned — from what to what, why?
4. Security incident you handled — what happened, how was it contained, what changed after?
5. How do you balance vendor relationships vs. internal builds?
6. Experience with [specific ERP / industry-vertical stack — depends on client]?
7. Comp expectation?

## Red flags
- Can't describe budget allocation
- No incident-response experience
- Heavy reliance on one specific vendor without rationale

## Green flags
- Clear vendor strategy
- Specific examples of cost-out programs
- Strong on cybersecurity (regardless of seniority)
