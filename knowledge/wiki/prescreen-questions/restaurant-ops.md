## Red flags (assistant flags these for Peter's manual review)

- Less than 12 months in current role
- Multiple job changes <18 months over the past 3 years (unless explained)
- Refusal to discuss reason for leaving
- Comp expectation >40% above the role band — likely not a real candidate
- Vague answers to specific operational questions (suggests not actually a fit)

## Green flags

- Specific numbers (units owned, AUV, COGS%, labor%)
- References named without being asked
- Has stayed 3+ years in roles
- Internal promotions in their history
- Has opened new markets / scaled regions
