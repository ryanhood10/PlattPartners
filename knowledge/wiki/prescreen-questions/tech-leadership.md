# Prescreen — Tech leadership roles

> **Status:** Skeleton. Replace with Peter's real client prescreens (especially for Director of DevOps, Director AWS Cloud, CIO, Senior Integration Architect — recent placement titles).

## When to use this

Director of Engineering, Director of DevOps, Director AWS Cloud, CIO, CTO, VP Engineering, Solutions Architect, Senior Integration Architect, Principal Engineer.

## Standard questions

1. Walk me through your current team — size, structure, what they own.
2. Describe a major architecture decision you owned in the last 18 months. What was the trade-off?
3. How do you balance build-vs-buy?
4. What's your hands-on/strategic split today (e.g., 30/70)?
5. Tell me about a hire you made that didn't work out. What did you learn?
6. What's your experience scaling a team from N to 2N? (matched to client's growth phase)
7. What stack are you most fluent in? What stack are you avoiding?
8. How do you measure engineering productivity?
9. Compensation expectation (base + bonus + equity)?
10. Earliest start if we move quickly?

## Red flags

- Cannot describe a specific technical decision in detail
- Buzzword-heavy answers without specifics
- "I oversaw" without "I personally did" examples
- No metrics — uptime, latency, deployment frequency, MTTR
- Has never managed a manager (if role requires it)

## Green flags

- Names specific tools, vendors, and reasons for choosing them
- Comfortable saying "I don't know but here's how I'd find out"
- Has shipped a major migration or scale event
- References available unprompted
- Healthy view of their own past mistakes
